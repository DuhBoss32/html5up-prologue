PokeMove

Embark on a Pokemon journey like never before with PokeMove! This innovative tool is a treasure trove for Pokemon aficionados, offering seamless access to move information through the PokeAPI. Dive into the world of Pokemon moves with ease, searching by name or type to uncover crucial details such as power, accuracy, and effects. Whether you're strategizing for competitive battles or exploring the vast Pokemon universe, PokeMove is your ultimate companion, enriching your experience with valuable insights and data-driven decisions.

Anime Online Database

Dive into the world of anime with the Anime Online Database project! This comprehensive tool harnesses the power of web scraping to gather detailed information from MyAnimeList. Stored in a MySQL database, this data is analyzed and transformed into captivating visualizations, providing invaluable insights into your favorite anime series and characters. Discover trends, explore genres, and gain a deeper understanding of the anime landscape through data-driven exploration with the Anime Online Database.

Riot Database

Join the league of data-driven gamers with the Riot Database project! This Python-powered application seamlessly gathers and processes player statistics and game-related information from Riot's API, storing them in a MySQL database. Utilizing libraries such as Cassiopeia and Pandas, this program provides deep insights into League of Legends gameplay, empowering players with valuable data for strategic decision-making. Dive into the world of data analytics and gaming with the Riot Database project!
